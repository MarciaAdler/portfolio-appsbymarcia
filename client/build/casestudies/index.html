HR Tech · AI/ML

The problem

Hiring managers were spending hours manually reviewing and verifying candidates. There was no intelligent layer to surface the verified candidates faster or reduce the burden on recruiters and hiring managers.

What I did

  • —Defined the end-to-end product vision and workflow for an AI/ML-powered candidate application, screening and verification system
  • —Scoped and integrated two third-party APIs to automate candidate data enrichment and verification
  • —Ensured model output accuracy through rigorous testing and quality benchmarks before launch
  • —Shipped a fully functional ATS with verification and screening built in — from zero to beta MVP
  • —Operated with a small team, owning strategy, requirements, and QA end-to-end

Outcome

< 3 months

Zero to shipped beta MVP

2 APIs

Integrated for automated data verification

0-to-1 · Full Stack

The problem

Property managers and tenants in smaller buildings (<50 units) were frustrated by the lack of ways to easily communicate with the community and each other about the happenings of the building. There was a clear gap for a focused, modern solution built around their actual workflows and providng a tool for these smaller buildings.

What I did

  • —Conducted customer interviews and market analysis to identify unmet needs and define the product scope
  • —Designed the full product architecture and roadmap from scratch — no team, no prior codebase
  • —Built the entire MVP full-stack using React, Node.js, and Express
  • —Validated the product with a real firm managing 100+ properties and potential users
  • —Iterated on the product based on user feedback

Outcome

< 4 months

Shipped the MVP and validated with a property management firm

Enterprise · M&A

The problem

Enterprise clients going through mergers and acquisitions needed a unified HR platform to manage people data across the combined future state organization — each with different systems, structures, and compliance requirements - in order to plan their future state org.

What I did

  • —Led end-to-end product lifecycle for an HR platform — from discovery and requirements through launch — across 5+ M&A clients in a year
  • —Ran enterprise discovery sessions and user interviews to translate complex stakeholder needs into prioritized requirements
  • —Designed and implemented RBAC, clean room protocols, and data-sharing restrictions to meet compliance requirements across clients
  • —Built and delivered Power BI dashboards surfacing org analytics to support executive decision-making during integrations
  • —Served as primary client contact, aligning executives and cross-functional stakeholders on product direction
  • —Mentored five junior PMs through delivery, contributing to $500K+ in new revenue

Outcome

5+ clients

Implemented in one year

$500K+
